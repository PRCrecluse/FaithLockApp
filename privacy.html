Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 17th April 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FaithLock ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

By using FaithLock, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect or store personal data like your name, email address, or IP address. However, certain information is collected automatically to improve the app's functionality and performance. This includes:

  • Crash logs (via Apple's diagnostics tools)
  • App usage data (via Apple, Superwall, and PostHog)
  • Device information (such as device model, iOS version, and language settings)

We also collect:

  • Religious preference (Christian-based prayer content)
  • Age group, as required for app personalization and experience

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this information to:

  • Monitor app performance
  • Diagnose bugs or crashes
  • Understand general user behavior (e.g., time spent in app, screen views)
  • Improve subscription experiences through Superwall

All analytics data is anonymized and is never used for advertising or sold to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party tools to operate the app:

  • Apple App Store & Analytics – for purchases and usage insights
  • Superwall – to manage subscriptions and present paywalls
  • PostHog (Cloud) – for product analytics and feature usage
  • Gemini API (Google) – to generate AI-based prayers

These providers may collect anonymized data according to their own privacy practices. No personally identifiable information is intentionally transmitted.

4. AI-Generated Content

FaithLock uses artificial intelligence to generate personalized prayers. These are created automatically based on your interaction with the app and are intended for devotional use only. No input is permanently stored or shared.

5. Your Privacy Controls

Since no user accounts are created, there is no user profile to delete. However, if you wish to request the deletion of analytics data associated with your device, contact us at:

prcrecluse@gmail.com

We will process your request and delete any identifiable analytics data stored via PostHog.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware of unintentional data collection from a child, we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes go into effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, reach out to us at:
